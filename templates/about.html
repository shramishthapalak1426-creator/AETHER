About AETHER

AETHER is an intelligent sentiment analysis dashboard designed to understand and visualize the emotional tone of online conversations. The platform analyzes comment sections from social media platforms such as YouTube and processes them using natural language processing techniques to determine whether the overall sentiment is positive, neutral, or negative. By presenting this data through interactive visualizations like charts, word clouds, and sentiment meters, AETHER helps users quickly grasp the general mood of an audience and the impact of online discussions.

In addition to sentiment analysis, AETHER includes an AI-assisted chatbot designed to interact with users in a supportive and conversational manner. The chatbot is built to recognize emotional cues in messages and provide thoughtful, uplifting responses, creating a more engaging and empathetic user experience.

This project was designed and developed by Palak Jain as part of a learning initiative to explore sentiment analysis, natural language processing, and interactive web dashboard development using Python, Flask, and modern web technologies.
